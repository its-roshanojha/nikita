2. Ride Related Issues
How to choose a pick-up time to reach the airport on time?
You can check the route travel time on Google maps to avoid getting late for the airport. It is advised to reach the airport 2 hours before for domestic flights and 3 hours for international flights.
How to share feedback?
You can give us feedback on the feedback link which is sent via WhatsApp on trip completion or you can share your feedback at care@bookairportcab.com.
How to get an invoice for my trip?
You need to download the invoice from the cab confirmation page or you can download it from the trip end message.
How to modify/edit my trip?
In case of any modifications in trip, a link is shared via whatsapp with which you can edit your trip.
Will the driver wait if the flight gets delayed or if I get late due to some unforeseen circumstances after landing?
The driver will wait for 20 mins without any extra charges and beyond that the customer will be charged depending upon the time taken.

3. Fare Related Issues
Why am I asked to pay extra in cash at the trip end for a prepaid ride?
The extra charges are only applicable if there is any toll or parking charges etc.
Why is my pay later ride more expensive than a prepaid ride?
When a customer books a prepaid ride then they are eligible for an exclusive offer. Hence, there is a difference in price.
Will I be charged for cancelling a trip?
If the booking is cancelled before the pick up time, you will not be charged for the cancellation and if cancellation takes place after boarding the cab there will be no refund.
What modes of payments are available?
We accept UPI, Credit & Debit Card, Net Banking, Paytm Wallet and cash as modes of payment.
Do riders have to pay extra for tolls or parkings?
Yes, the riders have to pay extra for tolls and parkings.
Is there a waiting fee?
There is no waiting charge till 20 minutes from the scheduled pickup time.

4. Refund Related Issues
In case the flight is cancelled/delayed or changes in plan . Will I get a 100% refund?
Yes, a customer is eligible to get a 100% refund if they cancel the ride before their pick up time.
